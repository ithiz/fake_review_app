How Detection Works

📝

Text Analysis

Scans review text for suspicious language patterns – excessive superlatives, repetition, urgency phrases, and hard-sell keywords.

⭐

Rating Pattern

Compares star rating against review sentiment to catch mismatches. A 5-star review with zero constructive feedback is a red flag.

👤

Reviewer Behaviour

Checks reviewer username patterns, sponsored disclosures, and writing style to flag bot-generated or incentivised reviews.

🤖

ML Scoring

Combines all signals into a fraud risk score (0–100%). Reviews scoring ≥ 40% are flagged as potentially fake.
